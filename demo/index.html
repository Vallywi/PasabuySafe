What the contract prevents.

🚫
Organizer blocks buyers after collecting money
Money never reached them. Buyers refund on-chain after the deadline.
🔐
Organizer fakes buyer confirmations
Confirming requires the buyer's private-key signature. Impossible to forge.
⛔
Organizer withdraws funds directly
The contract has no withdraw function. Only buyer-signed confirms release money.
📆
Organizer changes the deadline
Deadline is set once at creation and stored immutably on-chain.

If nothing arrives, refunds are automatic.

Deadline passes with no delivery? One click. Money returns to the buyer's wallet. No support ticket, no email, no approval — the contract enforces it.
