# PasabuySafe Demo Video Script

**Duration:** 3:30 – 4:00 minutes  
**Style:** Animated explainer + live product walkthrough  
**Tone:** Friendly, urgent, trustworthy — speaks to Filipino online shoppers  
**Music:** Upbeat Filipino-inspired lo-fi / modern pop instrumental (positive energy)  
**Resolution:** 1920x1080 (landscape) + 1080x1920 (vertical for socials)

---

## Pre-Production Notes

### Target Audience
- Filipino online shoppers (ages 18-40)
- Facebook/Viber/Telegram group buy participants
- People who have been scammed or fear being scammed in pasabuy
- Organizers who want to prove they're legitimate

### Key Message
> "Your money is safe until you confirm delivery. No more scams."

### Visual Style Reference
- Shopee/GCash commercial aesthetic (bright, friendly, mobile-first)
- Brand colors: Sunshine Yellow (#FACC15), Midnight Slate (#0F172A), Mint Green (#10B981)
- Typography: Plus Jakarta Sans (headlines), Inter (body)
